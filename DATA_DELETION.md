# Data Deletion — Donate Your Speech

**Developer:** UiT The Arctic University of Norway (Divvun)

## How to Delete Your Data

To request deletion of your donated recordings, follow these steps:

1. Open **Donate Your Speech**.
2. Tap **More Information**.
3. Go to **Privacy & Info**.
4. Under **Removing Your Recordings**, copy your **Client ID** using the "Copy Identifier" button.
5. Send an email to <feedback@divvun.no> with your Client ID and request that all your donations be removed.

Your Client ID is an anonymous identifier assigned to your app installation. It is required so we can locate and delete your data.

## What Data Is Deleted

When you request deletion, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

- All voice recordings you have submitted
- Recording metadata (duration, sample rate, timestamps, device information)
- Your anonymous client identifier

## What Data Is Retained

No data is retained after deletion. We do not keep backups of deleted recordings.

## Contact

If you have questions about data deletion, contact us at <feedback@divvun.no>.
